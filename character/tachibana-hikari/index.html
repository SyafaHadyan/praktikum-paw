橘ヒカリ

Introduction

Hikari is a member of the Central Control Center, the student affairs of Highlander. Despite her position, she tends to not take her work seriously and often causes trouble alongside her younger twin sister Tachibana Nozomi.

Personality

Hikari typically maintains a polite and calm demeanor, being soft-spoken. She usually likes being with her sister, Nozomi, who is her complete counterpart. Hikari doesn't really express herself, sometimes trying her best to follow Nozomi's behavior, mostly causing trouble. She also may not think much about the consequences of her actions until they actually happen, as shown in her relationship stories.

Appearance

Hikari is a petite young girl with hip-length, wavy light lime colored hair, with two waist-length hair strands. She has golden eyes and elf-like pointed ears. She has a black, heart-spaded tail, similar to Tanga Ibuki and as well her twin sister Nozomi.
